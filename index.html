Creative Philosophy

Work that feels
alive.

Creative success comes from alignment. A magic moment when shared vision, emotional investment, talent, and tenacity all come together. Those are the moments worth chasing.

My work is built around six pillars: creative collaboration, developing new works, nurturing emerging talent, authentic storytelling, deep artistic partnership, and a commitment to international creative exchange.

Whether I'm in a casting room in Brooklyn or a rehearsal hall in Bangkok, I bring the same belief: that the right people, connected to the right project, can create something truly extraordinary.

About Megan

Seasoned,
and passionate.

Megan Larche Dominick is a dynamic force in the global entertainment industry. A seasoned casting director, producer, and artistic consultant with over 20 years of experience spanning Broadway, film, and international theater. Her passion is developing new works and nurturing emerging talent.

Megan co-created The Ugly Xmas Sweater Musical, a heartwarming holiday show now licensed by Concord Theatricals. She is also a proud member of the Dramatists Guild and the Casting Society.

Currently she splits her time between Bangkok and Brooklyn, bringing a global perspective to every creative partnership.
