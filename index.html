My Approach

Therapy that feels
human.

You do not need to arrive here with everything figured out. You just need to arrive. My role is to create a space where your whole self is welcome, not just the tidy, presentable parts.

I draw from Internal Family Systems, attachment theory, and somatic awareness. In practice: we move at your pace, follow what matters to you, and build something real together.

Therapy here is not about fixing what is broken. It is about understanding yourself more deeply, so you can live with more ease, more presence, and more joy.

About Me

Experienced,
and human.

Hi, I am Maeve. I have been doing this work for over a decade, and I still believe the therapeutic relationship is where the real healing happens.

I trained in Edinburgh, completed my clinical hours in NHS settings and private practice, and have since worked with hundreds of people navigating anxiety, burnout, grief, and identity.

In sessions, I am warm, direct, and genuinely curious about you. I will laugh with you when that is what the moment calls for, and sit with you quietly when that is. Therapy here is not a performance. It is a real conversation.
